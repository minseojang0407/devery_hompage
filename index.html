About DEVERY

At Devery, we believe that everyone deserves access to the power of coding—especially individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD).
Through inclusive, project-based learning, Devery empowers the IDD community to learn, create, and thrive with technology.
Our logo shows one person teaching another at a computer—because at the heart of Devery is connection, support, and shared growth.
We’re not just building skills. We’re building bridges.
